SriLuxe Getaways - Ella Heights


Ella Heights at SriLuxe Getaways offers a retreat amidst nature's beauty. Relax in cozy rooms, savor delicious cuisine, and explore the lush Ella surroundings. Our hotel is the perfect base for trekking adventures and discovering the hill country.





SriLuxe Getaways - Galle Charm


Galle Charm at SriLuxe Getaways is a boutique hotel that combines colonial elegance with modern luxury. Our hotel offers charming rooms, a rooftop terrace with panoramic views, and easy access to Galle's historic attractions.

Trinco Serenity at SriLuxe Getaways is your gateway to peaceful coastal living. Enjoy comfortable accommodations, a beachfront setting, and water sports facilities. Immerse yourself in the marine wonders of Trincomalee's coastline.
